# Ore Distribution

Ores are a special feature type with distribution curves
that control where and how densely they appear.

# Elysium Ore Defaults

The Elysium profile uses these ore distributions:

Coal: Y 0-192, peaks around Y 96, common
Iron: Y -64-72, peaks around Y 16, common
Copper: Y -16-112, peaks around Y 48, moderate
Gold: Y -64-32, peaks around Y -16, uncommon
Lapis: Y -64-64, peaks around Y 0, uncommon
Redstone: Y -64-16, uniform distribution, moderate
Diamond: Y -64-16, concentrated at Y -59, rare
Emerald: Y -16-320, mountains only, very rare

# Diamond Scarcity

Diamonds use a triangle distribution heavily weighted
toward the deepstrip. The effective spawn rate drops
sharply above Y -48. Mining at Y -59 is optimal.

> In Elysium, diamond veins average 4-8 ores per vein
> with about 1.2 veins per chunk at optimal depth.

# Per-Biome Overrides

Override ore rates for specific biomes:

  [biome.mesa.ores.gold]
  count = 12
  min-height = -64
  max-height = 32

This makes gold twice as common in mesa biomes.

# Global Scarcity Multiplier

Apply a multiplier to all ore rates at the profile level:

  [profile.ores]
  scarcity-multiplier = 0.5

> A multiplier of 0.5 halves all ore spawns, creating a
> harder survival experience. Use 2.0 for generous ores.
